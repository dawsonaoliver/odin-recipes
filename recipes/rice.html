InstantPot Rice

Description

Delicous rice is always so hard to get right... :-( Not any more! 4 minutes in the InstantPot for the fluffiest of rice!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of White Rice
  • 2 Cups of Chicken Stock

Steps

  1. Place rice amd chicken stock in InstantPot
  2. Cook on High Pressure for 4 minutes. Allow pressure to release naturally.
  3. Serve!
